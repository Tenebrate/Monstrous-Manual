--------------------------
  NON-HOSTILE ELEMENTALS
--------------------------

Azer: A creature resembling a dwarf made out of brass. They are genderless, but possess innate natural skills at both metal- and gemcrafting. They also provide illumination in the dark, and have a heat aura that they can use in combat. Azers can be found rarely in any desert or in the deepest cavern layer, and can join civilizations.
